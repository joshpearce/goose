<objective>
Wire the `GooseUploadWatermark` store (from 62-01) into the live upload pipeline so uploads resume from the last confirmed point after a crash/restart, and add the logout/device-swap reset path.

Purpose: Replace the session-only `sinceTimestamp` (which resets to `now - 24h` on every restart) with a persisted, per-type effective lower bound. The watermark is read BEFORE each upload (used as the effective `since`) and written AFTER confirmed HTTP 2xx — separately for decoded streams and raw frames, because they can fail independently (RESEARCH Pitfall 3). Completes UPLOAD-WM-01.

Output: Modified `GooseUploadService.swift` (read/gate + atomic write on success) and `GooseAppModel+Upload.swift` (`clearAllUploadWatermarks()` reset helper).
</objective>
