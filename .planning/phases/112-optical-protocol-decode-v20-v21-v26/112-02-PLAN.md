<objective>
Add `DataPacketBodySummary::V20V21OpticalMultiChannel` variant and the supporting `OpticalChannel`
struct to protocol.rs, then parse packet_k 20 (2140B, 5-block i32) and packet_k 21 (1244B,
3-channel i16) out of the Unknown catch-all.

Purpose: WHOOP 5.0 bulk optical sensor frames (packet_k 20/21) are the primary source of raw
multi-channel PPG data used for SpO2 estimation and general waveform analysis. Currently they fall
silently to Unknown on every sync. This plan makes them structurally parseable so Phase 113 can
insert them into `optical_channel_samples`.

Output:
- New `OpticalChannel { index: u8, samples_i32: Option<Vec<i32>>, samples_i16: Option<Vec<i16>> }` public struct
- New `V20V21OpticalMultiChannel { version: u8, channels: Vec<OpticalChannel>, warnings: Vec<String> }` enum variant
- New `parse_v20v21_optical_body(packet_k: u8, payload: &[u8]) -> (Option<DataPacketBodySummary>, Vec<String>)`
- Match arms for packet_k 20 and 21 in `parse_data_packet_body_summary`
- Updated `data_packet_domain()` for packet_k 20 and 21
- Public test accessor `parse_v20v21_optical_body_for_test`
- Synthetic tests: v20 2140B (3 active channels), v21 1244B (3 channels)
</objective>
