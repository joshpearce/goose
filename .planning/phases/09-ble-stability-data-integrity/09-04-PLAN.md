<objective>
Apply the shared ReconnectBackoff exponential-backoff reconnection to the HR monitor BLE path (FIX-03), matching the WHOOP behavior delivered in Plan 03 — including cancellable scheduling and manual Stop/Try Again controls — and surface its state in ConnectionView.

Purpose: GooseBLEHRMonitorManager.didDisconnectPeripheral currently has zero reconnect logic — it sets hrPeripheral = nil and stops. This plan gives the HR monitor its own self-contained ReconnectBackoff instance (D-07), captures the disconnected peripheral before clearing it (RESEARCH Pitfall 4), schedules timed reconnect attempts via a stored, cancellable DispatchWorkItem plus a generation token (REVIEW HIGH — asyncAfter cannot be cancelled directly), publishes the reconnect state to ConnectionView via the WHOOP-parallel pattern, and adds HR-specific Stop/Try Again controls so ROADMAP success criterion 4 ("manual retry and stop at any time") is satisfied for the HR monitor as well (REVIEW HIGH).

Output: an hrReconnectState @Published property + helpers on GooseBLEClient, a backoff-driven cancellable HR reconnect loop in +HRMonitor.swift, HR Stop/Try Again forwarders, and an HR Reconnect row + controls in ConnectionView.

Depends on Plan 03 (the shared ReconnectBackoff struct must exist). autonomous: false — HR monitor reconnect requires a second physical BLE device, so the final verification is a human checkpoint per VALIDATION.md.
</objective>
