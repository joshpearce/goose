<objective>
Apply the shared ReconnectBackoff exponential-backoff reconnection to the HR monitor BLE path (FIX-03), matching the WHOOP behavior delivered in Plan 03, and surface its state in ConnectionView.

Purpose: GooseBLEHRMonitorManager.didDisconnectPeripheral currently has zero reconnect logic — it sets hrPeripheral = nil and stops. This plan gives the HR monitor its own self-contained ReconnectBackoff instance (D-07), captures the disconnected peripheral before clearing it (RESEARCH Pitfall 4), schedules timed reconnect attempts, and publishes the reconnect state to ConnectionView via the WHOOP-parallel pattern.

Output: an hrReconnectState @Published property + helper on GooseBLEClient, a backoff-driven HR reconnect loop in +HRMonitor.swift, and an HR Reconnect row in ConnectionView.

Depends on Plan 03 (the shared ReconnectBackoff struct must exist). autonomous: false — HR monitor reconnect requires a second physical BLE device, so the final verification is a human checkpoint per VALIDATION.md.
</objective>
